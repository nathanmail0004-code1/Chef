How booking works

Our booking process is simple and personal: tell us the date, headcount, and style of service. We’ll follow up to confirm menu details, dietary needs, and logistics.

  1. Request a booking using the button above or via our contact form
  2. We check availability and propose a menu
  3. Deposit, confirmation, and final details 7 days before service

Frequently asked questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes — we can adapt almost any menu for allergies, vegetarian, and other dietary needs with advance notice.

How far in advance should I book?

For private dinners, 2–4 weeks is typical; catering and larger events are best booked 6–12 weeks ahead.

Is travel included?

Within local service area travel is included; remote events may incur travel and overnight fees depending on distance.
